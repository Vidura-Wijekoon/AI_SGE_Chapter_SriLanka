What We Are Doing

  • Operationalizing Safety: Implementing the "Kill-Switch Protocol" and "Fairness Audits" across key sectors.
  • Building Capacity: Conducting workshops to train the next generation of AI Safety Auditors.
  • Regional Collaboration: Partnering with global entities to ensure Sri Lanka's AI stack is interoperable yet sovereign.
  • Public Awareness: Educating citizens on data rights and AI literacy via the "Punkalasa" initiative.
