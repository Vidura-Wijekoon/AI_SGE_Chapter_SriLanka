Our Mission

The Sri Lanka AI SGE Chapter exists to promote AI Safety, Governance & Ethics across Sri Lanka's technology, research, and policy community. We build awareness, foster critical discourse, and connect local practitioners with a global movement of 77+ chapter communities working to ensure AI benefits humanity — now and into the future.

Local Context

Why Sri Lanka, Why Now

🚀 Growing Tech Sector

Sri Lanka's IT and BPO industry is rapidly expanding, with AI adoption accelerating across finance, healthcare, and public services. The risks grow with the opportunities.

🏛️ Policy Gaps

National AI governance frameworks are nascent. Community-led advocacy plays a critical role in shaping responsible policy before harms manifest at scale.

🌐 Bridge Role

Positioned to connect South Asian and Global South AI safety communities, Sri Lanka can lead governance-first positioning in the Asia-Pacific region.
