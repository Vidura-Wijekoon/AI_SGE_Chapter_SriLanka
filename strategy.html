Why SGE?

Artificial Intelligence is a high-pressure resource, much like the water in our ancient reservoirs. Without regulation, it destroys. With the right "Bisokotuwa" (Valve Pit), it nourishes a nation.

The Digital Bisokotuwa

Just as the Bisokotuwa was an engineering marvel designed to regulate massive water pressure and protect the dam, our AI Safety, Governance, and Ethics (SGE) framework acts as the "Digital Bisokotuwa" for Sri Lanka. We are not building brakes to stop innovation; we are building "Trusted Rails" to allow high-speed innovation without derailing our social cohesion, national security, or cultural identity.

The "Muragala" Framework

1. Bahirawa (Foundation)

Sovereign AI Security

The "Hard Shell". Protecting the physical and digital infrastructure from adversarial threats. This includes Sovereign Compute and data sovereignty.

2. Nagaraja (Guardian)

Agile AI Governance

The "Operating System". Moving beyond bureaucracy to an agile "Hub-and-Spoke" model where the NCAI sets standards and sectoral regulators adapt them.

3. Punkalasa (Prosperity)

AI Ethics & Alignment

Ensuring AI serves our cultural values. This includes Linguistic Sovereignty (Sinhala/Tamil SLMs) and Fairness Audits to prevent algorithmic exclusion.

Strategic Alignment

Our safety interventions are directly mapped to the National AI Strategy 2028 pillars. For example, in Agriculture, we deploy "Red-Teaming" to prevent data poisoning. In Education, we mandate "Linguistic Sovereignty" to prevent cultural erasure. Safety is not red tape; it is the enabler of sustainable growth.
